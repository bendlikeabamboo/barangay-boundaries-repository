The updated masterlist can be accessed and downloaded at

The compilation and updating of the PSGC is a collaborative effort between the
Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) and its interagency TWGGC, which
includes the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), Department of Budget and
Management (DBM), Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG),
Land Management Bureau (LMB), Land Registration Authority (LRA), NHCP,
and the National Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA).
