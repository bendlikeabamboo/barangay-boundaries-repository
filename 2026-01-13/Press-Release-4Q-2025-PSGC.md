The compilation and updating of the PSGC is a collaborative effort between the

Commission on Elections (COMELEC), Department of Budget and
Management (DBM), Department of the Interior and Local Government (DUG), Land
Management Bureau (LMB), Land Registration Authority (LRA), National Historical
Commission of the Philippines (NHCP), and the National Mapping and Resource
Information Authority (NAMRIA).
