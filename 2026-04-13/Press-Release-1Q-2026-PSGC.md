Merging of Two Barangays, and Correction of
the Names of One Municipality and Seven Barangays

Barangay San Rafael in the City of Calaca, Batangas has been officially merged with
Barangay Dacanlao, in accordance with the Supreme Court decision in General Register
(G.R.) No. 234228 (Justo Q. Sinag, et al. v. The Honorable Sangguniang Panlalawigan ng
Batangas, et al.) promulgated on 25 February 2025.
The Supreme Court upheld the validity of the merger, confirming that Barangay San Rafael
was deemed abolished following a valid plebiscite in 1998. Accordingly, the geographic code
for Barangay San Rafael has been retired, and its territorial jurisdiction is now consolidated
under Barangay Dacanlao. This change has been officially adopted in the First Quarter 2026
Philippine Standard Geographic Code (PSGC).
Additionally, the names of one municipality and seven barangays in the PSGC masterlist were
corrected based on the recommendation of the interagency Technical Working Group on
Geographic Code (TWGGC). The details of the updates are as follows:

Authority (LRA), National Historical Commission of the Philippines (NHCP), and the National
